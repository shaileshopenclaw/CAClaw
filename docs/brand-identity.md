# CAClaw Brand Identity

This page explains how CAClaw should look and feel as a brand.

## Simple brand idea

CAClaw should feel:

- professional
- reliable
- calm under pressure
- suitable for tax, audit, compliance, and reporting work

It should not feel:

- playful in a way that reduces trust
- mascot-heavy
- visually similar to official ICAI branding
- like a separate full product that hides its OpenClaw connection

## Naming

Use these brand rules:

- **CAClaw** = the add-on name
- **OpenClaw** = the main platform name
- when needed, say **CAClaw for OpenClaw**

## Visual rule

Keep the visual style clean and professional:

- clear typography
- controlled colors
- strong readability on light and dark backgrounds
- simple icon or lettermark treatment

## Trust rule

A CA-facing tool should look serious enough for real office work.
That matters more than novelty.
