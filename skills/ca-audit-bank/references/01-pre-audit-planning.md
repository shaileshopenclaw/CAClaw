## PHASE 2: AUDIT PLANNING (SA 300)

### Materiality Determination

```
STEP 1: SELECT BENCHMARK
  For banks: Total Advances is the primary benchmark (most material item)
  
STEP 2: APPLY PERCENTAGE
  High NPA branch:      0.5% of Total Advances
  Normal branch:        0.75% of Total Advances
  Low-risk branch:      1.0% of Total Advances
  Alternative (P&L):    2-5% of Net Interest Income (NII)

STEP 3: CALCULATE
  Branch Advances (₹ Crore):           ___
  × Materiality % (e.g. 0.75%):        × 0.0075
  = Overall Materiality (OM):          ₹ ___ Crore
  × 75% = Performance Materiality (PM): ₹ ___ Crore
  × 5%  = Trivial Threshold:           ₹ ___ Crore

STEP 4: APPLY
  → MOC items > OM + bank disagrees = LIKELY QUALIFICATION
  → MOC items > PM: escalate to partner
  → MOC items < Trivial Threshold: can be ignored
  
RUN: scripts/03_regulatory_compliance_calculators.py → Module D
```
