## PART 5: INTEREST RATES & BENCHMARKS

```
EBLR (External Benchmark Linked Rate):
  Mandatory for: All floating rate retail + MSME loans (post Oct 2019)
  Benchmarks: RBI Repo Rate / 3M T-bill yield / 6M T-bill yield / SOFR
  Reset Frequency: Minimum quarterly
  Spread: Cannot increase post-sanction (only on credit risk changes)
  Current Repo Rate (FY 2024-25): check RBI latest announcement

MCLR (Marginal Cost of Funds Based Lending Rate):
  Applicable: Pre-Oct 2019 floating rate loans
  Components: Marginal cost of funds + tenor premium + operating cost + CRR cost
  Published: Monthly (overnight/1M/3M/6M/1Y/2Y/3Y tenors)
  Minimum tenure: overnight

BASE RATE (pre-2016):
  Still applicable for grandfathered loans pre-MCLR
  Should have migrated to MCLR/EBLR

PENAL CHARGES (RBI Circular Aug 2023):
  No "penal interest" (interest on interest)
  Only "penal charges" (flat fee) permissible
  Must be disclosed in loan agreement
  Cannot be higher for retail vs corporate for same default type
  Applicable from April 1, 2024

SOFR (Successor to LIBOR):
  USD LIBOR discontinued June 30, 2023
  SOFR (Secured Overnight Financing Rate) = replacement for USD
  PCFC/PSCFC ceiling: SOFR + 200 bps (verify current RBI circular)
```

```

  DICGC Insurance:                    ₹5,00,000 per depositor per bank

  Dormant Account:                    No transaction ≥ 2 years

  TDS rate (no PAN furnished):        20%
  Form 15G/15H:                       Exemption if total income below threshold
  TDS deposit to Govt.:               Within 7 days of end of month
  Form 26Q filing:                    Within 31 days of quarter end

KYC:
  Enhanced Due Diligence:             PEPs, High-risk, Countries
  Re-KYC Low Risk:                    Every 10 years
  Re-KYC Medium Risk:                 Every 8 years
  Re-KYC High Risk:                   Every 2 years
  Cash transactions requiring KYC:    ≥₹50,000 single transaction
  Cash transactions PAN required:     ≥₹50,000 or aggregate ≥₹10L/year
  CTR Filing (Cash Transaction):      ≥₹10 Lakhs / day (aggregate across branches)
  STR Filing (Suspicious):            Within 7 days of identifying suspicion
  LRS (Liberalised Remittance):       USD 250,000 per person per year
```
